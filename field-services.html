Case Study

How a 12-Person HVAC Company Transformed Their Operations

The Challenge

A growing HVAC company was running on paper work orders, losing track of appointments, and the owner was spending every Sunday evening doing invoices. There was no system for follow-ups — completed jobs disappeared into a filing cabinet, and repeat business depended entirely on customers remembering to call back.

Our Solution

We built a custom scheduling platform with AI-powered dispatch that matches techs to jobs by skill, location, and availability. We replaced paper with digital work orders featuring mobile photo capture and customer signatures. Invoicing was automated to trigger on job completion. Finally, we implemented customer follow-up and review request sequences that run on autopilot.

The Results

  • 20+ hours per week saved on administrative tasks
  • $180K revenue increase in year one
  • 60% fewer missed appointments
  • Google rating improved from 3.9 to 4.8 stars
  • Owner reclaimed weekends — no more Sunday invoicing
